# Computer+ Apps & Use Cases

Source: devrev.ai product & use-case pages. Verified 2026-03-31.

## Apps

**Support App** – AI-native support. Zero-touch across email/Slack/WhatsApp/Instagram. Auto-triage, KB gap detection, sentiment analysis. Stats: 85% auto-resolve, 50% cost reduction, 4x faster.

**Build App** – Ship products faster. Unites product/design/eng. AI auto-populates work items, routes issues, tracks progress.

**Observe App** – Product analytics. Session replays, funnel analysis, conversion optimization. Drag-and-drop funnels, cross-session journeys.

## Use cases (persona → pain → key stat)

- **Sales**: 75% time on admin → 6 hrs saved/rep/week, 30% productivity boost, ~3 weeks off cycles
- **Customer support**: 20+ tabs, buried knowledge → 40% faster resolution, 4x productivity
- **CX resolution**: High expectations, tight budgets → 70-85% auto-resolve, 15-week deploy
- **Service desk (IT)**: Routine tickets drowning IT → 60%+ instant resolve, 5 min avg (from 4 hrs)
- **Operations**: 15 tabs, manual updates → 6x efficiency, 50%+ RCA reduction

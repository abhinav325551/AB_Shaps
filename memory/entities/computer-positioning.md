# Computer – Positioning & Messaging

Source: devrev.ai homepage, blogs, product pages. Verified 2026-03-31.

## Company
- Founded 2020 by Dheeraj Pandey + Manoj Agarwal (Nutanix founders)
- 650+ employees, 8 offices, $1.2B+ valuation, HQ Palo Alto

## Hero messaging
- "Stop searching. Start knowing."
- "The only AI teammate with answers you can trust"
- "Team Intelligence" (not "AI")

## Core narrative
- AI-native vs bolt-on: "Imagine building a modern skyscraper on the foundation of an old, rickety shed."
- "Computer doesn't bolt AI onto an outdated architecture. It is the architecture AI needs."
- "Most teams aren't short on information. They're short on confidence."
- "Computer doesn't just read and summarize – it updates CRM fields, logs activities, creates tasks, routes approvals."

## Against competitors
- Positions against Zendesk, Salesforce, Intercom, Jira
- Edge: bidirectional CRUD (not read-only summaries)
- "Only 28% of companies unlock real value from customer service AI" (BCG)

## Proof points
- 85% issues resolved automatically
- 50% support cost reduction
- 10+ hrs saved weekly/employee
- 40% faster ticket resolution
- 25% customer retention increase
- 4x team productivity

## Key brand phrases
- "A living, breathing, digital twin"
- "Code: optional. Context: automatic."
- "Every day you wait, a competitor's graph deepens instead."
- "Technology should serve people, not the other way around."

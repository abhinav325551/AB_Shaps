## Logo Placement

## Slide Layouts
1. **Title/cover**: Full-bleed color bg, large bold title bottom-left, logos top
2. **Content**: White bg, thin rule at top, title top-left, body right 2/3
3. **Big number**: Left white + right colored block for stats
4. **Content + device**: Left text + right colored block with device mockup
5. **2-column**: Left white text + right full-height color panel
6. **Speaker/bio**: Colored bg, circular headshot, white text
7. **Thank you**: Full-bleed color, large product wordmark cropped at bottom
